#BLACKLIVESMATTER


To our peers, friends, family, and community
In light of recent events, we decided it was only right to pause on the further development of outreach and spend some time reflecting, learning, collecting resources, and sharing actionable items that can help all of us better ourselves and our communities. We want to start by saying that the student-led GD Committee stands in solidarity with the Black Lives Matter movement. As visual communicators, we recognize the power that comes with our chosen paths and want to use our voice to help support our friends, our communities, and the Black Lives Matter movement. We value equality, diversity, and inclusivity, and as individuals are working towards better educating ourselves for the fight against systemic racism.

Below is a list of resources that we encourage everyone, especially all white and non-black POC to spend some time reading through, to reflect, learn, and take a stand with actions that make the most sense to each of us. Additionally, we have highlighted the graphic design graduates below who are black and encourage all visitors to uplift black voices in their community as well.

Now is not the time for silence and complacency. We must all do our parts in raising awareness, supporting, and uplifting our black community to help get the justice we all deserve. We hope you will stand with us to support our friends and family part of the black community.

Fran, Aini, & Deb
The GD Gradex Committee
